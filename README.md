### About CH-MJ LAHQ
**CHINESE MJ LOS ANGELES HQ** is a global family built for Chinese-speaking Michael Jackson fans around the world. Connected by our shared love for MJ, our community continues to grow and welcome new members every day.

Beyond hosting various tribute events and celebrations, we are deeply committed to carrying on MJ’s legacy of love and compassion. We strive to do our utmost to give back through charitable initiatives and community service—honoring his spirit by making a positive difference. Just as MJ taught us to *"Heal the World,"* we look forward to coming together with you to make the world a better place.

*"It's all for love."* 🌻
